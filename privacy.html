Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to PagiQ. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how PagiQ ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, protects, and handles your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, and related PDF solutions (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details when you register for a PagiQ account.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our app, such as features accessed (e.g., scanning, converting, editing) and time spent, to improve performance.
  • Device Information: Hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure compatibility and troubleshoot issues.
  • User Content: Files (such as PDFs, images) that you choose to process using our AI tools or cloud conversion features. Note: Files processed entirely offline remain on your device and are not collected by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Services (e.g., performing AI summaries or cloud conversions).
  • To process transactions and manage your premium subscription.
  • To improve and personalize user experience.
  • To communicate with you, including sending updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.

3. Data Processing and AI Features

PagiQ utilizes Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide smart features like document summarization, translation, and interactive chat. When you use these features:

  • Your documents may be temporarily processed on our secure servers.
  • We do not use your personal documents to train our AI models unless you explicitly opt-in.
  • Processed data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and is deleted from our servers shortly after processing is complete.

4. International Privacy Rights

PagiQ operates globally and complies with major international privacy frameworks. Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

Europe (GDPR & UK GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you have the right to:

  • Access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your data.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format).
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

United States (CCPA / CPRA & State Laws)

If you are a resident of California or other applicable US states, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is being collected and how it is used.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. Note: PagiQ does not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

China (PIPL)

If you are a resident of the People's Republic of China, we process your personal information in compliance with the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL):

  • We will obtain explicit consent before processing sensitive personal information or transferring data cross-border.
  • You have the right to know, decide, restrict, and refuse the processing of your personal information.
  • You have the right to request the deletion or correction of your data.

Rest of the World

We extend foundational privacy rights (access, correction, and deletion) to all our users globally, regardless of their location, in accordance with applicable local laws (e.g., LGPD in Brazil, APPI in Japan).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including end-to-end encryption for cloud transfers and strict access controls, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Services (e.g., cloud hosting providers, payment processors). These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
